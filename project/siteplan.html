Michael Holmes

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to show the different products that Holmes Hardware has made, and demonstrate the skill and proficiency of the main carpenter Matthew Holmes. This website will also allow the user to contact the company to request a custom peice to be made.

Audience

The intended audience will primarily be the residents of Waynesboro, Virginia and those in surrounding areas such as Stuarts Draft, Staunton, Crimora, and Augusta County.
